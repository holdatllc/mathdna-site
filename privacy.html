Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026 · Energea Technologies LLC

MathDNA is built to be private by design. It has no user accounts and does not collect personal information. There are no analytics SDKs, no advertising, and no third-party trackers.

What stays on your device

Your progress — XP, streak, reasoning-move mastery, topic accuracy, and badges — is stored only on your device. If you turn on iCloud classroom sync, that data lives in your own iCloud account (Apple CloudKit) and is not accessible to us.

What the app sends to our server

To keep the question library fair, the app talks to our question service only when you download more problems. In that request it sends:

  • a random device identifier — a UUID generated on first launch, not linked to your name, email, Apple ID, or any personal information — used only to enforce the monthly fair-use limit and to avoid sending you the same problem twice; and
  • if you subscribe, an App Store transaction receipt supplied by Apple, used only to confirm an active subscription.

We do not receive your name, email, location, contacts, photos, or advertising identifiers.

Subscriptions

MathDNA Pro is an auto-renewing subscription sold and processed by Apple. All payment information is handled by Apple — we never see your payment details. Manage or cancel anytime in the App Store under Settings → your name → Subscriptions.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with third parties for advertising. Your usage, progress, and reasoning data stay on your device or with our own problem-library service.

Optional answer-checker (Google Gemini)

MathDNA includes an optional, off-by-default feature that checks your final answer. It works only if you add your own Google AI Studio (Gemini) API key in Settings. When you use it, the text of the problem you typed is sent directly from your device to Google (generativelanguage.googleapis.com, over HTTPS) so Gemini can solve and check it, under Google's API terms; it is never sent to us. Your API key is stored only in your device's Keychain. Without a key, nothing is sent to Google and the free reasoning check still runs entirely on-device.

Children's privacy

MathDNA is designed for learners of all ages. Because it collects no personal information and requires no account, it is safe for classroom and family use, and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

The app works fully offline with its built-in library — if you never download more problems, it never contacts our server. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.
