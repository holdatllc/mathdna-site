Getting started

  • Choose Student or Teacher on first launch.
  • In Solve, pick a problem and write your solution one step at a time — MathDNA types each step (setup, real work, conclusion) and shows where the reasoning holds or breaks.
  • Journey takes you through belts as your reasoning improves.
  • Bring Your Own lets you type any homework problem and get a reasoning check with no answer key needed.

Frequently asked

Does MathDNA need the internet?

No. 19,000 problems and all grading run on your device, offline. A connection is used only if you choose to download additional problems (Pro) or use the optional answer-checker.

What's free, and what's paid?

The app is free to use: 19,000 problems, step-level grading, the Journey, and a taste of competition/olympiad — all offline. Optional subscriptions (MathDNA Pro and Max Pro) let you download more problems each month, including the full competition/olympiad library. Manage or cancel anytime in the App Store under Settings → your name → Subscriptions.

What is the optional answer-checker?

MathDNA always checks your reasoning for free. If you add your own Google AI Studio (Gemini) API key in Settings, it will also solve the problem and check your final answer. Your key stays in your device's Keychain and the request goes straight to Google — never to us. See the Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my data?

Everything you create (progress, saved work) lives only on your device. Deleting the app removes it all. MathDNA requires no account.

Where do the problems come from?

Practice problems are redistributed from openly licensed datasets (OpenMathInstruct-2, NuminaMath, Orca-Math, GSM8K) with attribution in the app under Settings → Acknowledgements.
