MathDNA grades the reasoning steps of a math solution — not just the final answer — and builds a “Reasoning DNA” of your strengths and weak spots so you can practice exactly what needs work.

Getting started

  • Choose Student or Teacher on first launch.
  • In Solve, pick a problem, write your solution one step at a time, then tap Grade my thinking for step-level feedback and partial credit.
  • Use Sharpen a weak spot and the Topic / Reasoning-move filters to drill the areas you’re weakest in.
  • Watch your growth in My DNA.

MathDNA Pro

The app ships with 19,000 verified problems that work fully offline. MathDNA Pro unlocks the full library — thousands more, up to 5,000 fresh problems a month. Manage or cancel anytime in the App Store (Settings → your name → Subscriptions).

FAQ

  • Do I need an account? No — MathDNA has no accounts and collects no personal information.
  • Does it work offline? Yes, the built-in library needs no connection.
  • How do I restore a purchase? Settings → Problem library → Restore Purchases.
